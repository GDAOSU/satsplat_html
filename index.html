SatSplat

Jiyong et al.

Satellite Remote Sensing & Computer Vision Group

Abstract

We present SatSplat, a novel framework for large-scale 3D satellite reconstruction. By integrating neural rendering with generative diffusion models, our approach overcomes the limitations of traditional stereo pipelines. SatSplat achieves high-fidelity geometry and realistic texture synthesis from multi-view satellite imagery.

Methodology

The pipeline consists of two primary stages. First, we optimize Gaussian Splatting for satellite geometry. Second, we apply a generative refinement process to recover fine-grained details and consistent illumination.
